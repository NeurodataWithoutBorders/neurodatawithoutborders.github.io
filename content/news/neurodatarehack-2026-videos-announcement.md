The recorded talks from [NeuroDataReHack 2026](/events/hck26-2026-janelia-ndrh/) are now available on YouTube. This was the fifth annual NeuroDataReHack, a week-long workshop hosted at the Janelia Research Campus of the Howard Hughes Medical Institute from July 12 to 17, 2026. Participants learned how to find, read, and analyze open neurophysiology datasets shared in the Neurodata Without Borders (NWB) format on the DANDI Archive, and they spent most of the week working on their own reanalysis projects.

## About NeuroDataReHack

NeuroDataReHack focuses on generating new insights from existing neurophysiology data through secondary analysis. The workshop emphasizes analyzing data that is already in NWB format and available on DANDI, rather than converting new data. This lets participants validate findings across species and brain areas, apply analysis tools to established datasets, explore follow-up questions to published studies, and create educational content from open data.
