Implementation Notes

To align buttons at the bottom of cards:

  1. Make sure the .card-body has the styles: 
    display: flex; flex-direction: column; height: 100%;
  2. Wrap your buttons in a .card-actions container: 
    margin-top: auto; padding-top: var(--space-md);
  3. The margin-top: auto pushes the container to the bottom of the flex container
  4. The cards will maintain equal height in the grid while keeping buttons aligned at the bottom
